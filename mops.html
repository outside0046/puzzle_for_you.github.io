Pug breed

The Pug's comical face, with deep wrinkles around big, dark eyes and a flat round face
can't help but make you smile.
Pugs are clowns at heart, but they carry themselves with dignity. Pugs are playful dogs,
ready and able for games, but they are also lovers, and must be close to their humans.
Pugs love to be the center of attention, and are heartsick if ignored.
